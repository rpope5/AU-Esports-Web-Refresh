# Recruit Triage Playbook (Phase 1)

This score is a decision-support signal for coaches and admins. It helps order review queues; it does **not** auto-accept or auto-reject recruits.

## Default Score Bands (All Games Except Smash)

- `>= 90`: **Very high confidence**
  - Shortlist candidates when coach bandwidth is limited.
- `>= 80`: **High priority**
  - Strong fit indicators; review first for immediate outreach or tryout consideration.
- `70-79`: **Review soon**
  - Promising but mixed signal; review after high-priority queue.
- `< 70`: **Low priority / backlog**
  - Lower current fit signal; keep in backlog and revisit as team needs shift.

## Smash Exception (Intentional)

Smash uses lower score bands because score distribution is compressed lower in current scoring outputs:

- `>= 60`: **High priority**
- `50-59`: **Review soon**
- `< 50`: **Low priority / backlog**

## Coach Usage Guidelines

- Use score bands to prioritize who to open first in admin triage lists.
- Use score breakdown/components to understand why a recruit scored where they did.
- Combine score with notes, role needs, and roster context before making final decisions.
- Do not treat score as an automatic outcome label.
